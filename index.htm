FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is this really free?

Yes. There's no cost and no sign-up — you can start chatting right away.

Can I chat without turning on my camera?

Yes, the text chat panel works independently, so you can keep your camera off if you'd rather just type.

Can I choose who I talk to?

Matching is random, but you can narrow the pool by country using the filter above the chat.

What happens if I click Stop?

The current call ends immediately. Click Start again whenever you're ready for a new match.
